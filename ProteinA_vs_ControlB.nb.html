14A. Hypothesis testing: non-parametric Kruskal-Wallis ANOVA

Note that the difference is important in order to have a working protein. The higher the ratio, then the protein indicates that the cell compartment is basic, and the other way around for acidic conditions.

H0: The means between groups are identical H3: At least, the mean of one group is different

kruskal_pA <- kruskal.test(log_ratio ~ treatment, data = combined_pA)
 # p < 2e-16*** (at least the mean of one group is different)
